How It Works

A Research Workflow for Multi-Source News Understanding

Perspective is built around a simple premise: understanding improves when the same event is examined across many sources, with differences made visible rather than averaged away.

1) Multi-Source Aggregation

Reporting on the same event is collected from a wide range of public news organizations. Articles are grouped by event to enable direct comparison across outlets.

2) AI-Assisted Summarization

Natural language processing techniques distill coverage into concise summaries that preserve core facts while reducing cognitive overload.

Summaries are designed as entry points ‚Äî not replacements for original journalism.

3) Framing & Perspective Surfacing

Differences in tone, emphasis, and interpretation are surfaced explicitly. This encourages readers to compare narratives and form independent judgments.

Sources & Coverage

Source diversity is a deliberate methodological choice. Perspective aggregates reporting from a broad, regionally distributed set of reputable news organizations to avoid reliance on any single editorial worldview.
