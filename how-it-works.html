How It Works

From raw data to balanced insights – a peek behind the scenes.

Our Process

1

Collect & Clean

We continuously gather news articles from trusted publishers. Our pipeline extracts the main content and removes clutter to prepare clean text for analysis.

2

Chunk & Embed

Articles are split into manageable segments and transformed into vector embeddings. This numeric representation captures the semantic meaning of the text.

3

Cluster Stories

Using similarity search and clustering, related articles are grouped into unified stories. This helps us detect when multiple sources are discussing the same topic.

4

Summarise & Compare

Large language models generate concise summaries and identify where viewpoints diverge. We surface key facts, tone, and framing differences across sources.

5

Rank & Present

Stories are ranked based on relevance, recency and diversity of perspectives. You receive a balanced feed with transparent analysis, ready for you to explore.
