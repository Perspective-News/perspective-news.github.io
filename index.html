The Problem

65 million people strive to follow news about India and the USA, yet nearly 70 percent feel they don’t know enough about the stories they care about. News is scattered across countless sites, headlines are sensationalized and biased, and it’s hard to piece together the full picture. This leads to confusion, mistrust and ultimately disengagement.

Our Solution

Perspective is your AI‑powered companion for news. We aggregate stories from across the web, summarize the facts, surface different angles and highlight what’s missing—so you can make up your own mind. Our platform cuts through the noise, giving you quick access to unbiased information and contrasting viewpoints in one place.

Features

Short Summaries

We condense lengthy articles into concise, fact‑checked summaries so you can stay up to date in minutes—not hours.

Balanced Feed

Our algorithm surfaces multiple sides of every story. Explore different framing, tone and emphasis to see where viewpoints converge or diverge.

Personalized Reading

Tell us what you care about and we’ll recommend the most relevant stories. Your reading list adapts as your interests change.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower every person to make informed decisions by providing clear, comprehensive and impartial news. We envision a world where access to balanced information fosters understanding, empathy and collective progress.
