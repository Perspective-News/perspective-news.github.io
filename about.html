About

Perspective

Perspective is a multi-source news reader that clusters articles into shared events and summarizes them—so you can understand what’s happening without relying on a single outlet.

Vision

To reshape how the world understands itself—by uniting voices, bridging borders, and delivering every story through an unbiased, human lens.

What you’ll find here

  • Region- and language-filtered feeds powered by a JSON data file.
  • Top stories ranked by number of sources, plus compact cards for everything else.
  • Source transparency via a one-click list of linked outlets.
