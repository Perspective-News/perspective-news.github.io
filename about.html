About Perspective

Our story, why we started, and where we’re going.

The Problem We’re Solving

In today’s hyper‑connected world, following important stories across borders is harder than ever. Millions of people want to understand news from the United States and India, but almost two‑thirds of them feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of articles, sensational headlines, and biased takes. Key details get buried, important context goes missing and it’s exhausting to piece together a complete picture.

Why Perspective?

Perspective was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves clear, comprehensive information to make informed decisions. We set out to build a platform that cuts through the noise, bringing together diverse sources, summarising the facts, and exposing the ways stories are framed. With AI assisting our curation, we hope to empower you to see beyond a single headline and form your own opinions.

Our Vision for the Future

We’re just getting started. In the coming months you’ll see curated feeds tailored to your interests, deep‑dives on trending topics, transparent analysis of biases and tone, and interactive tools to explore differing viewpoints. Our mission is to become the world’s most trusted destination for multi‑perspective news.
