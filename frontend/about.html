Understanding News Beyond a Single Narrative

Perspective is a research-driven platform exploring how multi-source aggregation and AI-assisted summarization can support clearer, more balanced news understanding.

The Problem

Contemporary news consumption is shaped by algorithmic curation, fragmented media ecosystems, and polarized framing. Readers are often exposed to isolated narratives rather than a full view of the same event.

  • High volume, low synthesis
  • Framing differences hidden in plain sight
  • Difficulty comparing coverage across outlets

What Perspective Studies

Perspective examines how presenting parallel framings of the same story influences comprehension, trust, and critical engagement. Instead of collapsing coverage into one ‚Äúfinal‚Äù narrative, the platform makes divergence explicit.

Framing signals

Surface differences in emphasis, tone, and interpretation.

Open Research Prototype

Perspective is developed as an open-source research prototype to support transparency, reproducibility, and community contribution. It functions both as a public interface and as a testbed for studying bias-aware summarization and responsible AI in media systems.
