How Perspective works

The product groups coverage around shared events and presents a single, readable summary with transparent sourcing.

1

Ingest articles

Articles are collected from multiple outlets and normalized into a consistent story format.

2

Cluster by event

Coverage is grouped into shared topics/events so you can compare perspectives without noise.

3

Summarize

A concise summary is generated for each cluster, optimized for quick understanding.

4

Show sources

Click the source count to view linked outlets for transparency and further reading.

5

Filter by region and language

Use the top ribbon to switch editions and languages; stories update instantly.
